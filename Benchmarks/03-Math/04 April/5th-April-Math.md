## 🧠 Key Idea
- Redundancy, contradictory & complete attributes don't matter now, what matters is singularity and non-singularity. -- >**A Geometric Notion of singularity**
- Redundant and contradictory systems of equations are fundamentally the same, Singular and the remaining one is singular --> **A Geometric Notion of singularity**
- The Co-efficients of the elements of systems of equations can be stripped out and put it in a box organized in rows and columns can form a matrix. ---> **Singular Vs Non-singular matrices**
- A **matrix** is simply an array of numbers inside a rectangle, arising naturally from the coefficients of linear equations. ---> **Singular Vs Non-singular matrices**
- The nature of the system of equation determines the nature of the matrix that was derived from the equation. ---> **Singular Vs Non-singular matrices**
- A matrix can be linearly **dependent** if the one or two rows can form the third using multiplication or averaging or addition or subtracting. --> **Linear dependence & independence**
- For a matrix to be Linearly Independent, it's rows cannot have any relation to each otherh that could form another row. --> **Linear dependence & independence**
