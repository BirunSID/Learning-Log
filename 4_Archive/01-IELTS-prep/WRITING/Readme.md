# IELTS writing system : The Manual

# Task 1: 4 block architecture
150 words at least

### Introduction: Paraphrase the main question in one sentence.
### Overview: Identification of the main trends of the task without an mention of data directly. 
### Detail paragraph 1: Group data set A , first half or 75% of all the primary data
### Detail paragraph 2: Group data set B , Last remaining information or major trends.

## Logic models:

### Comparative
**Charts, tables, pie , graph** . *focus on magnitude, difference, and rank*

### Sequential
**Process diagram** . *Focus on stages and transformations.*

### Spatial
**Maps** . *Focus on change in location/layout.*


# Task 2: Rational Argumentation
The goal is to build a high-level academic argument. (250 words at least)
## structural blueprint:
### Introduction:
	1. Hook (paraphrase the main topic)
	2. Thesis statement (my direct answer)
	3. outline (what I shall discuss)
### Body paragraph 1
- **P**oint: Clear topic sentence.
- **E**xplanation: Deepen the logic.
- **E**xample: Concrete evidence (studies, examples, logic).
- **L**ink: Re-connect to your thesis.
### Body paragraph 2
- **P**oint: Clear topic sentence. 
- **E**xplanation: Deepen the logic.
- **E**xample: Concrete evidence (studies, examples, logic).
- **L**ink: Re-connect to your thesis.

### Conclusion:
Summary of main points . There shall be no new information.


***I must be consistent with the information that i provide. There can't be any contradictions.***

# Task 2 types:
## opinion(Agree/Disagree):
- MUST TAKE A CLEAR SIDE , NO MIDDLE GROUND!
## Discussion(Both views):
- Give equal weight to both sides before explain why my side is superior.
## Advantages Vs. disadvantages:
- similar to opinion, but focused on weight again, must conclude with which side is heavier here.
## Problem and solution(Or cause and effect):
- Direct cut to chase about the fix to the problem, no need for personal opinion unless, asked.
## 2 part - direct questions:
- there will be two unrelated questions
- treat them as 2 new mini paragraphs and that's it.
