# Achievement & Milestone Archive

## Purpose
This directory is the "Hall of Fame" for the repository. It serves as the final destination for major life goals, long-term projects, and certifications that have been **successfully completed.** 

While the other folders in this repo track ongoing growth, the Archive is a record of **results.**

---

## Directory Structure
Items are moved here only once they have reached their final milestone or "Definition of Done."

```text
Archive/
├── 📂 IELTS-Band-8.0/        # Language proficiency documentation and results.
└── 📂 Finished-Books/        # Comprehensive reviews of life-changing literature.
```

---

## 📜 The "Archive" Rules
To maintain the prestige of this directory, the following standards apply:

1. **Definition of Done:** Only projects that have reached their target (e.g., a specific score, a certificate, or a finished book) are moved here.
2. **Post-Mortem Documentation:** Every archived folder should include a final summary of the experience and the key takeaways learned during the journey.
3. **No Active Work:** If a project is still being updated or studied, it belongs in the `Knowledge_Vault` or `Projects_and_Labs`. 
4. **Historical Integrity:** Files in this folder are rarely edited; they serve as a permanent record of past achievement.
