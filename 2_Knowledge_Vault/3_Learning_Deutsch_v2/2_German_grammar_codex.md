## Logic & Frameworks

### 1. The 4 Cases (Kasus)
- **Nominativ:** Subject (Der/Die/Das)
- **Akkusativ:** Direct Object (Den/Die/Das)
- **Dativ:** Indirect Object (Dem/Der/Dem)
- **Genitiv:** Possession (Des/Der/Des)

### 2. Verb Position (V2 Rule)
- In a standard statement, the conjugated verb is **ALWAYS** the 2nd element.
- *Example:* "Ich **trinke** Wasser." vs "Heute **trinke** ich Wasser."

### 3. Personal Pronouns
| Subject   | Verb (Example) |
| :-------- | :------------- |
| Ich       | -e             |
| Du        | -st            |
| Er/Sie/Es | -t             |
